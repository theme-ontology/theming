sex work
========

:: Description
A character works voluntarily and gainfully in either providing
direct sexual services, i.e. prostitution, or with activities that
center around indirect sexual stimulation, such as erotic dancing
or producing pornography.

:: Parents
human occupational activity

:: References
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sex_work


coma
====

:: Description
A character is in a coma, i.e., a deep state of prolonged unconsciousness from which they cannot be awakened, fail to respond normally to painful stimuli, light, or sound, lack a normal wake-sleep cycle and do not initiate voluntary actions.

:: Parents
human health condition

:: References
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coma
