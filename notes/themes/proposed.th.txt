
conjoined twins
===============

:: Description
There are a pair of twins that were physically joined in utero, that is, before they were born.

:: Examples
In tftc1989e2x17 "My Brother's Keeper", were literally joined at the hip. Their combined epidermis merged there, and they shared a circulatory system. The doctor gave them each a 50/50 chance of surviving a separation procedure, should they choose to undergo it.
