what if I was trapped in a work of fiction
==========================================

:: Description
A character experiences what it is like to be inside the world painted by the
story of a creative work (e.g., a novel, movie, poem, etc.), with no way to
escape it.

:: Parents
what if I found myself in a strange place

:: Examples
Commander Riker's landing party was trapped in an eternal live enactment of a
second rate 21st century novel in tng2x12 "The Royale".
