movie: Men in Black 3 (2012)
============================

:: Title
Men in Black 3

:: Description
In the film, Boris the Animal, an old enemy of Agent K, escapes from prison and travels back in time to kill the younger K in order to allow his species, a ruthless alien race known as Boglodites, to attack Earth. This forces Agent J to go back in time and team up with K's younger self to save his partner and the world. It is the third installment in the original Men in Black film series which in turn is loosely based on the comic book series The Men in Black by Lowell Cunningham.
