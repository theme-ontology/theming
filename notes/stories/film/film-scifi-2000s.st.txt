movie: Pitch Black (2000)
=========================

:: Title
Pitch Black

:: Description
Pitch Black (titled The Chronicles of Riddick: Pitch Black on its DVD
re-release) is a 2000 American science fiction action horror film directed by
David Twohy and co-written by Twohy and brothers Ken and Jim Wheat from a
story conceived by the latter.

Synopsis: The dangerous criminal Richard B. Riddick is being transported to
prison in a spacecraft. When the spaceship is damaged by comet debris and
makes an emergency crash landing on an empty desert planet, Riddick escapes.
However, when predatory alien creatures begin attacking the survivors, Riddick
joins forces with the surviving crew and other passengers to develop a plan to
escape the planet.

:: Date
2000-02-18

:: References
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pitch_Black_(film)

:: Ratings
2 <paul>

:: Major Themes
what if I were stranded on a deserted planet [The story follows the efforts of the surviving crew and passengers of a crash-landed spaceship to get off a desert planet that was inhabited by hoards of man-eating creatures.]
desert planet [A main novelty of the film is the baked in perpetual daylight by three suns desert planet on which it is set.]
human vs. nature [The surviving crew and passengers of the Hunter-Gratzner spaceship struggled to survive on the desert planet on which they crash-landed.]
trust in a potential foe [Carolyn and Johns took a calculated risk by releasing the dangerous criminal Riddick and accepting him into their party. They remained suspicious and mistrustful of him throughout the course of the film, but needed his help to survive on the planet.]
human vs. monster [Central to the story was the surviving crew and passengers of the Hunter-Gratzner spaceship trying to not get killed by the man-eating creatures that inhabited the desert planet on which they'd crash-landed.]
monster [The desert planet was inhabited by grotesque, man-eating creatures.]
